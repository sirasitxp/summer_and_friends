Our Story

PetPaws Grooming started as a small home‐based salon in San Francisco. Founded by lifelong animal lovers, we set out to create a calm, professional environment where every cat and dog is treated like family.

Our Mission

To provide stress‐free grooming experiences using only gentle techniques and top-quality, pet-safe products—so your furry friend always leaves feeling comfortable, clean, and confident.

Our Values

  • Care: We prioritize your pet’s comfort and well-being.
  • Quality: Premium tools and products for superior results.
  • Integrity: Transparent pricing and honest advice.
